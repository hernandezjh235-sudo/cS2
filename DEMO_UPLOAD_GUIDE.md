# Demo Upload Guide

Open **Data Manager → In-App Demo Center**. Upload `.dem`, `.zip`, `.csv`, `.json`, or `.jsonl` files and press **Parse + Ingest Demo Data**. Files with complete round tables feed map KPR, CT/T splits, economy states, role signals, and five-player team allocation. Kill-only exports never change KPR unless a complete map-round denominator is provided.

For automatic ingestion, put files in `/data/cs2_engine/incoming_demos` on the shared Railway volume.
